Louis Vuitton SS26 Bags & Favourite Pieces

Louis Vuitton SS26 bags and the full collection were presented on September 30th, 2025 in the north wing of the Musée du Louvre in Paris.

Louis Vuitton SS26 bags and the full collection were presented on September 30th, 2025 in the north wing of Musée du Louvre in Paris, more precisely within the summer apartment of Anne of Austria. And Home is where I want to be, spoken by Louis Vuitton's ambassador Cate Blanchett at the start of the runway show.

The collection embraces the intimacy one experiences when dressing up for oneself, at home. "The atmosphere I was wishing to share with you was really this serenity that you can feel when you are in the comfort of your home," Louis Vuitton's creative director Nicolas Ghesquière told the press after the show.

Keep on reading to discover more of this beautiful collection and my favourite pieces that I was kindly invited to experience during an intimate viewing in Brisbane last week.

From the iconic summer bag-to-be, the Louis Vuitton Whisper clutch set to go viral across socials, to the newly launched Squire bag, Louis Vuitton SS26 bags have us excited to plan our spring coffee dates and summer getaways.

And whilst I have been loving the entire collection (discover my favourite pieces further below!), there has been one look I simply cannot stop thinking about. The moment I saw it move on the runway, I knew I had to try it on. I might not be engaged (yet) but eloping in Paris is my version of a dream 'wedding'. This perfection of a blazer with the silky Bermuda shorts (just mentally add a pair of white heels) would be the ultimate courthouse outfit, what do you think?

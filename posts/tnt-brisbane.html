TNT The New Trend
Opens on James Street, Brisbane

Published 20th October 2025 · By @dom.overseas

My favourite luxury Australian fashion destination, TNT The New Trend, has opened the doors to its luxury boutique on James Street in Brisbane. A Queensland first, it is perfectly located within The Calile James Street shopping precinct.

Their carefully put-together brand curation not only makes the perfect excuse for a staycation in the city perfected with a shopping spree — it is also set within the most incredible interior design by AKI.

You adore refined tailoring with a unique twist? Discover an evening with TNT & Christopher Esber here.

Brisbane's latest high-end fashion boutique — TNT The New Trend is now open

From Melbourne and Sydney to Brisbane

If you are like me and much prefer shopping in person, the stylists at TNT The New Trend in Brisbane have you covered. From sought-after global brands The Attico, Coperni and Wardrobe NYC to Australian and NZ brands Maison Essentiele, Solaqua Studio, Harris Tapper, Christopher Esber and Courtney Zheng, you will find the most beautiful pieces that cannot be missing from your wardrobe.

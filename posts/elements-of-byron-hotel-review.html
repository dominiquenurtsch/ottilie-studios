Byron Bay Hotel Review – Elements of Byron

Add the luxury hotel Elements of Byron Bay to your Australian hotel list!

Add the luxury hotel Elements of Byron Bay to your Australian hotel list! For my birthday weekend we decided to head down to nearby Byron Bay and combine work with pleasure. I was kindly invited by Slojourn Studio, a conscious travel communications agency, to check in and experience the beautiful luxury Australian hotel and all it has to offer and create some content for the hotel channels.

The expansive beach-front property is located just behind the Byron Industrial Estate which is home to some of my favourite local stores and cafés. Add browsing the beautiful St. Agni store and a coffee at next-door coffee shop Tones at Soko to your itinerary!

Nestled between the beach and the rainforest, this award-winning luxury accommodation is the perfect retreat from everyday life. Our 2-bedroom villa was looking out onto the rainforest and the nightly rain showers were so soothing, we slept almost 10 hours! No YouTube rain sounds required here.

We spent a sunny day by their adults-only pool and indulged in their pool-side menu. Usually those tend to be underwhelming but the Greek Salad with Tzatziki and Lemon Chicken has to be one of the most flavour-some meals we have had this year!

If you are looking for a barefoot luxury Byron Bay getaway outside of the busy town close to nature yet only a short drive away from all the hustle and bustle, Elements of Byron is an excellent choice!

About Dominique

Dominique (@dom.overseas) is an Australian social media content creator known for her refined minimalist aesthetic and globally engaged audience. She partners with luxury hotels, global fashion houses and beauty brands to deliver high-converting visuals, destination storytelling and elevated brand narratives that resonate across Australia, the United States and Europe.

She travels frequently to Paris Fashion Week, producing editorial content for both luxury and high-street brands. Her work spans fashion, beauty, travel and lifestyle — blending effortless style with modern storytelling to create high-impact digital content, UGC and premium campaign assets that brands confidently use across their marketing.

The Work

What makes this content convert?

UGC (User-Generated Content) is purpose-built content created for brands to use across their marketing — from paid ads and websites to organic social. It performs because it feels genuine, relatable and trustworthy compared to traditional advertising.

Dominique produces elevated, aesthetic, natural-feeling content that aligns with your brand identity while speaking directly to your audience — full service from concept development and scripting through to shooting, editing and delivery of campaign-ready assets.

Whether you need luxury fashion visuals, travel footage, beauty routines or lifestyle storytelling — the output feels organic and converts.

Common Questions

Everything brands need to know.

How do brand partnerships with Dominique work?

Every collaboration starts with a brief or rate card request. Dominique reviews the brand fit, agrees on deliverables (posts, UGC assets, usage rights, timeline), and produces content aligned with your campaign goals. Long-term partnerships are available as retainers with priority access and preferred rates.

What content categories do you cover?

Fashion (styling, editorial, runway), beauty (flat-lay, routine, application), travel and hotel (atmosphere, design detail, guest experience), and lifestyle (interiors, food, daily life). All content carries Dominique's signature refined, minimalist aesthetic.

Can we use the content for paid advertising?

Yes — UGC packages include full licensing for paid media use. Whitelisting (running paid ads through Dominique's account) is also available, giving your brand access to her engaged audience for Meta and TikTok campaigns.

Do you work with international brands?

Absolutely. Dominique's audience spans Australia, the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany and Europe. She travels frequently to Paris, Italy, Mallorca and beyond, making her well-suited to international campaigns, brand trips and destination content.

What is the best way to get in touch?

Email dominique@ottilie-studios.com.au with your brand name, campaign brief and timeline. A rate card and availability can be sent within 24–48 hours.

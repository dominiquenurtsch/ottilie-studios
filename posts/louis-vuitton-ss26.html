Louis Vuitton SS26 bags and the full collection were presented on September 30th, 2025 in the north wing of Musée du Louvre in Paris, more precisely within the summer apartment of Anne of Austria. It was a show that reminded the audience why the French house continues to lead the conversation on understated, highly crafted fashion.

An ode to Parisian elegance — Nicolas Ghesquière's tenth anniversary

Celebrating ten years as creative director of Louis Vuitton women's

The Louis Vuitton SS26 runway marked Nicolas Ghesquière's tenth anniversary at the helm of women's — and the house celebrated with a collection that felt both personal and archival. Modern silhouettes met embroidered jackets, tulle blouses, and bags that are already showing up on every Parisian arm I've seen this season.

Keep on scrolling for a first look at my favourite bags and pieces from the Louis Vuitton SS26 collection.

Favourite Louis Vuitton SS26 bags

The SS26 bag line-up leans into softness — rounded hardware, structured shoulder shapes and a return to the monogram in muted palettes. A few pieces are already calling: a gently slouched top handle, a mini bucket, and a revived envelope clutch in caramel.

Final thoughts on Louis Vuitton SS26

SS26 for Louis Vuitton is a collection about quiet confidence — pieces that will slip into wardrobes and stay there. If you are building a luxury bag collection slowly, the SS26 silhouettes are the ones I would be planning for.

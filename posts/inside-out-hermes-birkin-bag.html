First Look at The Inside-Out Hermès Birkin Bag

Bonjour Birkin à l'envers! The classic Hermès Birkin bag was unveiled in an eye-catching new design at the Parisian SS25 showing.

Bonjour Birkin à l'envers! The classic Hermès birkin bag was unveiled in an eye-catching new design at the Parisian SS25 showing — translating to "upside down Birkin".

The Hermès artisans craft the bags traditionally from the inside out before finalising the last steps in this meticulous process.

This bold and modern new design was inspired by "skipping" this last step and leaves it "inside out", highlighting the iconic Birkin Bag features.

Byron Bay Hotel Review – Elements of Byron

A birthday weekend at one of Australia's most beautiful beachfront properties.

A Weekend of Barefoot Luxury

Elements of Byron is a luxury beachfront property nestled among the rainforest near Byron Bay. We stayed in a 2-bedroom villa with rainforest views — and I slept almost 10 hours every night thanks to the sound of the rain on the canopy.

The resort is perfectly positioned — far enough from town to feel like a genuine escape, but close enough that you can wander in for a browse or a coffee whenever the mood strikes.

The Pool, The Food, The Feeling

The adults-only pool is a dream. We ordered a poolside Greek salad that was honestly one of the most flavoursome meals we'd had all year — simple, fresh and effortlessly good. That's the magic of Elements of Byron. Everything feels considered without feeling fussy.

If you're looking for barefoot luxury in Australia — somewhere that balances nature with genuine comfort — this is it. It's the kind of place you arrive at and immediately wish you'd booked an extra night.

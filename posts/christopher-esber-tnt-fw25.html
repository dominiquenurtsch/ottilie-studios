Christopher Esber and The New Trend, a Love Story for the Ages

TNT · The New Trend and Christopher Esber have built one of Australian fashion's most consistent and meaningful partnerships. The label's razor-sharp tailoring and quiet luxury aesthetic feels perfectly at home within The New Trend's carefully curated edit — and the Fall/Winter 2025 collection is the strongest expression of that alignment yet.

Celebrating 15 Years of Christopher Esber

The intimate cocktail event at Bar Le Splendide in Melbourne marked a milestone — 15 years of Christopher Esber. The space was perfectly chosen: refined, moody and architectural, much like the collection itself. I wore the Mineral Stone Cascade Long Sleeve Dress with a red lip, which felt exactly right for the occasion.

Christopher Esber Fall/Winter 2025, Now at The New Trend

The pieces I couldn't stop looking at: the Devon Leather Bomber Jacket in burgundy — the kind of piece that immediately reads as investment — and the Tuxedo Vest, which can take an outfit from considered to exceptional with one layering decision. The Fall/Winter 2025 collection is now available through The New Trend Australia.

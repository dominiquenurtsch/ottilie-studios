Louis Vuitton Beauty — a natural progression in the French house's history

Dame Pat McGrath, DBE — Creative Director, Cosmetics for La Beauté Louis Vuitton

Louis Vuitton's vision is deeply rooted in travel, creativity and savoir-faire. The thoughtfully curated make-up line offers not only elevated beauty products — from the finest quality make-up to luxurious lipsticks — they are also housed in objets d'art, little luxuries, to be kept forever.

The House created this incredible new line in partnership with the iconic Dame Pat McGrath, DBE, as Creative Director, Cosmetics for La Beauté Louis Vuitton. Together, they invite the world to discover a universe of beauty through innovation, emotion, and luxury. More than just beauty products, this new launch forms the expression of identity, artistry, and empowerment.

Louis Vuitton Beauty as a lifestyle

La Beauté Louis Vuitton is more than just elevated beauty products. The House redefines beauty as a lifestyle experienced as a daily luxury. From refillable lipstick cases to keepsake packaging, this is a launch that has us — and our bathroom shelves — very excited.

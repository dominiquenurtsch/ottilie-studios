Overview

All products sold on this website are digital products delivered electronically — including downloads, templates, guides and online content. Due to the nature of digital products, our refund policy is as follows.

Change of Mind

We do not offer refunds for change of mind once a digital product has been accessed or downloaded. By completing your purchase, you acknowledge that you are purchasing a digital product and agree that access to the product constitutes delivery.

Your Rights Under Australian Consumer Law

Nothing in this policy excludes, restricts or modifies any rights you may have under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL). You may be entitled to a refund if the product:

  • Has a major defect or fault
  • Does not match its description or our representations
  • Is not of acceptable quality

Defective Products

If you believe your product is defective or not as described, please contact us within 14 days of purchase with the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Your order number or proof of purchase
  • A description of the issue you are experiencing

We will review your request and respond within 2–3 business days. Where a defect is confirmed, we will offer a replacement, corrected file or refund at our discretion.

Access Problems

If you are unable to receive or access your purchase — for example, if you did not receive your download email — please contact us before requesting a refund and we will do our best to resolve the issue promptly.

How to Submit a Refund Request

To submit a refund request, please email us with:

  • Your name
  • Your order number or proof of purchase
  • A clear description of the issue

Requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis in line with our obligations under Australian Consumer Law.

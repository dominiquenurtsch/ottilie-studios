The Craft

What is a content creator?

A content creator is someone who builds stories, visuals and experiences that resonate with a specific audience — and for me, @dom.overseas, it's about documenting a blend of creativity, strategy and lived experience. I create daily fashion, travel and lifestyle content across Instagram, TikTok and my blog, partnering with luxury and high-street brands, hotels and tourism boards while travelling between Australia and Europe.

A digital content creator doesn't just create pretty images; we research, plan, shoot, edit and create narratives that inspire action, drive brand awareness and build community. In my work, that means producing high-quality editorial imagery, city guides, hotel reviews, outfit videos and long-form storytelling that brands can confidently use across their marketing.

UGC Social Media Content

UGC (User-Generated Content) is purpose-built content created by real users, customers or creators for brands to use across their marketing — from ads and websites to social media. This type of content performs exceptionally well because it feels genuine, relatable and trustworthy compared to traditional advertising.

As a UGC creator, I produce high-quality videos and images that brands can license for paid media, product launches, testimonials, tutorials and lifestyle storytelling. My focus is on elevated, aesthetic, natural-feeling content that aligns with your brand identity while speaking directly to your audience — concept development, scripting, shooting, editing and delivery of ready-to-use assets tailored to your campaign goals.

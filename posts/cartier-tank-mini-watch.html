New In: Tank Mini Model Louis Cartier Watch

The micro-watch trend meets Cartier's most iconic silhouette — and the result is perfect.

Why the Tank Mini Model Louis Cartier Watch Needs To Go On Your Wrist

The Tank Mini Model Louis Cartier watch in yellow gold is precisely what the micro-fashion trend looks like translated to fine watchmaking. Launched in September 2024, its 24mm x 16.5mm case sits perfectly on smaller wrists — delicate without being precious, and unmistakably Cartier.

The quartz movement is housed beneath a case finished with Cartier's signature Roman numerals and blue sapphire crown. It's the kind of watch that makes every outfit feel more considered — whether that's a relaxed weekend look or a sharp evening ensemble.

How I Am Styling the Cartier Tank LC Mini Watch

I've been layering it with my Cartier Love bracelet and a simple Love ring — the yellow gold tones throughout the stack feel cohesive without looking matchy. During the day, I'm wearing it with oversized blazers and straight-leg denim. For evenings, it pairs beautifully with something sleeveless that lets the wrist do the talking.

The Tank silhouette has been on wrists since 1917. This mini version feels like its most wearable iteration yet.

Polka Dot Baignoire Watch

The Cartier Polka Dot Baignoire watch first premiered at Watches and Wonders 2025 and is quickly set to become the it-girl watch of 2026.

The Cartier Polka Dot Baignoire Watch introduces a playful new expression of one of Cartier's most elegant watch silhouettes. Staying true to the iconic Baignoire oval form, this new design keeps the dial clean and understated, allowing the decorative diamond polka-dot detailing to take centre stage. The result is a watch that feels distinctly Cartier — timeless in form, with a strong unique design.

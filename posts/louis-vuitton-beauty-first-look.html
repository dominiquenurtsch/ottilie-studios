Louis Vuitton Beauty: A First Look

The most anticipated beauty launch of the year — and it lives up to everything.

Louis Vuitton Beauty, a Natural Progression in the French Luxury House's History

La Beauté Louis Vuitton represents more than just elevated beauty products — it functions as a lifestyle experience. Developed in partnership with Dame Pat McGrath, DBE as Creative Director, Cosmetics, the collection aligns with Louis Vuitton's core values centred on travel, creativity and savoir-faire.

Dame Pat McGrath, DBE, as Creative Director

Appointing Dame Pat McGrath as Creative Director is a statement in itself. One of the most influential forces in beauty globally, her involvement means this collection is not simply a brand extension — it's a genuine creative vision. Every product reflects her philosophy: beauty as transformation, beauty as art.

Louis Vuitton Beauty as a Lifestyle

What sets La Beauté Louis Vuitton apart is its packaging as much as its formulas. Each product is designed as a keepsake — the kind of object you leave on your vanity rather than tucking away in a drawer. The luxury extends past the product itself into the ritual of using it.

Matte Lipstick in Rouge Louis and Lip Baume in Tender Bliss

The two products I've been wearing most are the Matte Lipstick in shade Rouge Louis — a deep, refined red that feels thoroughly Parisian — and the Lip Baume in Tender Bliss, which sits at the other end of the spectrum entirely: sheer, nourishing and effortlessly wearable. Together they cover every occasion, which feels very much by design.

Why Dominique

Refined visual storytelling, built on 15 years of experience.

Dominique (@dom.overseas) is an Australian–German content creator whose work has been trusted by Chanel Beauty, Hermès Beauty, Louis Vuitton, Tory Burch and New Balance. A former Fashion Assistant at VOGUE in Munich and Paris, she brings an editorial eye to every piece of content she creates — whether it's a hotel suite at dawn or a cobblestone street in the 1st arrondissement.

Her audience of 53K+ on Instagram (12.8M+ monthly reach) skews 91% women, aged 25–44, concentrated in Australia, the US and Europe — the exact customer luxury hotels, resort brands and destination tourism boards want to reach. She doesn't just post and move on — she creates lasting visual assets brands return to for months.

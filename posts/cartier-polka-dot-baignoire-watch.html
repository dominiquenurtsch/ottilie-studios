New In: Cartier Polka Dot Baignoire Watch

A playful new expression of one of Cartier's most elegant silhouettes — and the ultimate elevated wardrobe staple.

Why We Love The Polka Dot Baignoire Cartier Watch

The Cartier Polka Dot Baignoire premiered at Watches and Wonders 2025 and represents a genuinely unexpected turn for the Baignoire silhouette — a watch known for its oval case and quiet elegance. The addition of decorative diamond polka-dot detailing gives it a playful character that still feels very much in keeping with Cartier's DNA. It's the ultimate elevated staple — something that works as well with denim as it does with a cocktail dress.

How I Am Styling the Polka Dot Baignoire Cartier Watch

My default stack has been the Polka Dot Baignoire layered with a Love bracelet and a thin chain bracelet in yellow gold — the tones complement each other beautifully without competing. For more casual days, I'm wearing it alone against the wrist, which allows the detailing to speak for itself. It pairs effortlessly with basics: a crisp white shirt, wide-leg trousers, minimal jewellery elsewhere. The watch does the work.

It's one of those pieces that looks far more expensive than it lets on — the diamond detailing catches the light in the most discreet way. Deeply wearable, thoroughly Cartier.

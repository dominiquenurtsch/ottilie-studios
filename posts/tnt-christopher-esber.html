Launches Christopher Esber FW25

Published 20th October 2025 · By @dom.overseas

My favourite luxury Australian fashion destination TNT The New Trend hosted an intimate cocktail hour with Christopher Esber at Melbourne's latest hotspot bar, Le Splendide.

With French champagne flowing and birthday cake served for 15 years of one of most successful Australian designers, it was an evening for the books.

Usually one to not wear much make-up in everyday life, I adore how Australian, Sydney-based designer Christopher Esber's work speaks to a woman who moves easily between occasions. I chose to add a red lip — such as Louis Vuitton's La Beauté lipstick in a dark red — to the Mineral Stone Cascade Long Sleeve Dress.

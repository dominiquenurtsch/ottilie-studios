Louis Vuitton SS26 Bags & Favourite Pieces

Presented at the Musée du Louvre on September 30, 2025 — and the bags are already on my wishlist.

Louis Vuitton SS26 Bags — Say Hi to the New Summer It-Bag

The SS26 collection was presented at the Musée du Louvre in Paris, with Creative Director Nicolas Ghesquière centering the collection around a feeling — "the serenity that you can feel when you are in the comfort of your home." The result is a collection that feels intimate and considered: softer silhouettes, familiar codes revisited with a quieter hand.

Monogram Is So Back (Even Though It Never Went Anywhere)

If the last few seasons saw logomania give way to stealth wealth, SS26 brings the Monogram back with confidence and without apology. The new Whisper clutch is a particular highlight — minimal in form but unmistakably Louis Vuitton in spirit. It's the kind of bag that works as a standalone statement or tucked under an arm at an event.

The newly launched Squire bag is also worth your attention — a structured silhouette that feels new within the LV universe but immediately coherent. I can already see it becoming a staple in the same way the Neverfull and Speedy did before it.

Take a Closer Look at the Louis Vuitton SS26 Collection

Beyond bags, the SS26 jewellery — particularly the LV Cube collection — feels like a natural extension of the collection's architectural sensibility. Geometric, precise and designed to layer. The show confirmed what we already knew: Louis Vuitton under Ghesquière consistently delivers collections that feel both current and timeless.
